Websites don't have to be complicated

I'm not the first to notice, websites are getting rather complicated.

Everyday browsers are frightened by the jargon and sheer amount if choice there is when it comes to building a website.

Back to basics websites - built with pure untainted HTML5 with no classes or styles. With limits, comes creativity.

Take a look at the source - really - it's not hard. Just right click, view source (usually). I'll bet you can understand my code, and write it yourself. Why not?
